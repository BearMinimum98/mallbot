What's MallBot?

MallBot is a simpler way to play the market game in KoL - pick your boozes, set your spending limit, and go!

How does MallBot work?

MallBot is built on top of PyKol, and is hosted by Google AppEngine.

How to use this amazing site?

Enter your username and password for KoL, then enter how much meat you are willing to spend on each type of booze. The undercut is how much less you want to price your booze than the current lowest price. For best results, run once a day when you haven't spent any adventures or brought anything from the mall yet. I am not responsible for any lost items or booze.

Help! The booze I want isn't listed!

For booze additions, please kmail KevZho (#2434890).

Does MallBot store any of my user information?

No. If you don't believe me, check out the GitHub repo, which has the source.
